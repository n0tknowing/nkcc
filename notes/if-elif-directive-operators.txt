- Valid operators on `if` and `elif` directive. Ordered by precedence:
     Operators  |   Associativity
    -------------------------------
     + - ~ !    |   right to left
     * / %      |   left to right
     + -        |   left to right
     << >>      |   left to right
     < > <= >=  |   left to right
     == !=      |   left to right
     &          |   left to right
     ^          |   left to right
     |          |   left to right
     &&         |   left to right
     ||         |   left to right
     ?:         |   right to left
     ,          |   left to right
- Expression only accepts integer constant and char constant, possibly defined
  by any previous macros.
